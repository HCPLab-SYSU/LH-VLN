The Fetch robot in simulator is a versatile mobile robot designed for logistics, material handling.
Action: Fetch support three kinds of action: move_forward, turn_left and turn_right.
Sensors: Fetch is equipped with three RGB cameras at a height of 1 meter in front, left and right to obtain embodied images in these three directions.
Mobility: Fetch movement is realized by a wheeled chassis, which can only move on flat ground and cannot pass through rugged areas.
Use: Fetch has a flexible robotic arm with a high position (1 meter) that can perform complex operations such as grabbing.
